# Anti-Patterns

## Banned Copy

Do not use:

- "We are excited to announce"
- "Unlock the power of"
- "Take your business to the next level"
- "Seamless experience"
- "Revolutionize"
- "Game-changing"
- "Empower your journey"
- "Dear valued customer"
- fake urgency such as "Act now" unless there is a real deadline in the input
- generic introductions that could fit any product
- multiple competing CTAs

## Banned Visual Patterns

Do not generate:

- one giant paragraph inside one `mj-text`
- decorative gradient blobs or complex background art
- fake badges with no purpose
- dense multi-column layouts for transactional emails
- product header/footer duplication
- fragile CSS, scripts, forms, or unsupported interactivity
- image-dependent layouts where the email fails without images

## Banned Data Behavior

Do not invent:

- legal claims
- discounts
- deadlines
- security events
- product features not implied by the prompt
- personal data variables that the sender may not have
